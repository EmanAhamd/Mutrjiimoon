LOCALIZATION

Localization is simply explaining to your client/customer their need for your product.

Localization is simply explaining to your client/customer their need for your product or service in their own language and dialect and according to their culture's norms. Thus, localization is "molding" the translated material into the local client's language, culture, and mentality. This necessarily entails understanding:

  • The product's nature
  • The country's language, culture, terminologies, and needs
